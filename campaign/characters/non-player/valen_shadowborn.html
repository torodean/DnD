Background Information

Valen Shadowborn is a skilled rogue who grew up on the streets and learned to survive by any means necessary. He specializes in sneaking, stealing, and staying alive in dangerous situations. Valen is known to take risks and is always looking for ways to make a quick profit. He has a quick wit and a silver tongue, which helps him get out of trouble more often than not. However, he has a tendency to be selfish and can sometimes put himself above his allies.

Notes

Valen Shadowborn is not to be trusted.
