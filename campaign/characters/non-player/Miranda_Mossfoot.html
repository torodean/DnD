Background Information

Miranda Mossfoot is the co-owner of Dragon’s Roost Inn, a cozy and welcoming inn located near the heart of the Ryumon District . Alongside her husband Finn Mossfoot, Miranda ensures that the inn offers a comfortable stay and hearty meals to all its guests. Known for her warm smile and attentive nature, Miranda takes great care in making sure that every guest feels like family. Her organizational skills and friendly demeanor contribute significantly to the inn's reputation as a beloved destination for travelers and adventurers.

Notes

Miranda's background as an innkeeper and her natural charisma make her an excellent host. She oversees the daily operations of the inn with efficiency and grace, ensuring that everything runs smoothly. Her cooking is as renowned as Finn's, with guests often praising the delicious meals she prepares. Miranda's dedication to her work and her ability to make everyone feel welcome have helped Dragon’s Roost Inn earn its reputation as one of the best places to stay in the Ryumon District .
