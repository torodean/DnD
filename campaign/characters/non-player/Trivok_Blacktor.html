Background Information

Trivok Blacktor is the younger brother of Ravik Blacktor and a trusted lieutenant within the Emberkawa Syndicate . Known for his lethal efficiency and cold demeanor, Trivok carries out his brother's orders with precision and loyalty. Trained as an assassin, he excels in the art of silent killing and covert operations, making him a valuable asset in the Syndicate's more clandestine activities. Trivok's relationship with his brother is complex, marked by a blend of loyalty, rivalry, and mutual respect. While he lacks Ravik's charisma and strategic brilliance, Trivok makes up for it with his ruthless effectiveness and unwavering dedication to the Syndicate's cause.

Notes

Trivok often operates in the shadows, handling the Syndicate's dirtiest work and eliminating threats before they become problems. His loyalty to Ravik is absolute, and he will stop at nothing to protect and advance the Syndicate's interests.
