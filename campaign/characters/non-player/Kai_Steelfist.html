Background Information

Kai Steelfist is a formidable street fighter who has earned his reputation through countless brawls in the back alleys and underground arenas of the city. Combining the disciplined techniques of a monk with the raw power of a fighter, Kai has honed his body into a weapon. His lightning-fast reflexes and brutal efficiency make him a force to be reckoned with. Despite his rough exterior and chaotic lifestyle, Kai has a code of honor and fights to survive, not just to win. He makes his living through street fighting, often participating in high-stakes matches that attract shady spectators and gamblers alike.

Notes

Kai wears a simple bracelet made of woven leather, a reminder of his humble beginnings and the friends he made (and lost) on the streets. He also tends to wear a mask to hide his face from the public.
