Background Information

Lysander Starweaver is a charismatic and flamboyant half-elf wizard who specializes in the art of illusion. With a natural talent for performance and a flair for the dramatic, Lysander has become a renowned entertainer in many a noble court and bustling city. His spells often combine dazzling visuals with intricate storytelling, captivating audiences and leaving them in awe. Despite his light-hearted demeanor, Lysander takes his craft seriously, constantly refining his techniques and seeking new ways to blend magic with art. His charm and wit make him a beloved figure, both on and off the stage.

Notes

Lysander always carries a beautifully crafted lute, which he uses to accompany his magical performances, blending music and illusion to create unforgettable spectacles.
