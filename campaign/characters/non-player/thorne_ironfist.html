Background Information

Thorne Ironfist is a dwarf fighter who has seen his fair share of battles. He is a skilled warrior and a natural leader, who is often put in charge of his group when on missions. Thorne is fiercely loyal to his comrades and will go to great lengths to protect them. He has a gruff exterior but has a soft spot for those in need. Thorne is a proud dwarf and is always up for a good ale and a hearty meal.

Notes

Thorne Ironfist has a tattoo of a warhammer on his bicep, which he got after a particularly memorable battle.
