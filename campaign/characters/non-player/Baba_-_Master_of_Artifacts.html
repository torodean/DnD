Background Information

This version of Baba has dedicated her life to the collection and study of ancient artifacts. Her keen mind and deep understanding of magical history have led her to uncover countless lost relics. She is known for her ability to manipulate time to her advantage, making her a formidable opponent and a valuable ally.

Notes

Baba, the artifact collector, is always in search of the next great discovery. Her vast knowledge of ancient civilizations and their magical creations makes her a sought-after expert in the field of artifact study. Despite her small stature, her presence commands respect, and her intellect is unmatched.

Abilities

  • -Time's Echo: Baba can create a temporal duplicate of herself for 1 minute. This duplicate can act independently, sharing her knowledge and spells.
  • -Chrono Shift: Baba can manipulate time to briefly rewind it. Once per day, she can rewind time by 6 seconds (1 round), allowing her or her allies to redo actions.
  • -Temporal Stasis: Baba can place a creature in a state of suspended animation. The creature is incapacitated, immune to all damage, and does not age. This lasts for up to 24 hours or until Baba ends it.
  • -Artifact Seeker: Baba has advantage on all checks to identify and locate magical artifacts. She can sense the presence of magical artifacts within a 1-mile radius.
  • -Arcane Knowledge: Baba's extensive study of artifacts grants her proficiency with Arcana and History, with double proficiency bonus for both.
  • -Future Sight: Baba can peer into the future and gain insights into upcoming events. As a bonus action, she can grant herself advantage on all attack rolls, saving throws, and ability checks for 1 minute. She can use this ability twice per long rest.
  • -Divine Nocro-Fusion: Upon a final death, An alternate version of Baba appears. This alternate version of Baba is divine and absorbs the current Baba into her being without explanation.
