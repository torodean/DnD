Background Information

Celestia Starweave is the enchanting owner of The Astral Attire in Vifavea, a celestial fashion boutique that captures the essence of cosmic elegance. With her innate sorcerous abilities and a keen eye for celestial fashion, Celestia curates a collection of enchanting garments infused with celestial magic. Her boutique is a sanctuary for those seeking to embrace their inner radiance and shine like stars.
