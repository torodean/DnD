Background Information

Doran Ironshield is a stout and resolute dwarf paladin, known for his unwavering commitment to justice and his unbreakable will. Raised in a clan of warriors, Doran's training as a soldier and his devotion to his deity have made him a formidable protector of the weak and a relentless foe to the forces of evil. His plate armor and shield are emblazoned with holy symbols, a testament to his faith and his duty. Doran's imposing presence and his righteous fury on the battlefield are matched only by his compassion and sense of duty off it.

Notes

Doran carries a small, intricately carved stone from his homeland, which he uses during his prayers to feel connected to his roots.
