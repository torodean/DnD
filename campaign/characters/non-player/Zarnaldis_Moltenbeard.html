Background Information

Zarnaldis Moltenbeard, the son of Aethur Moltenbeard, ascended to the throne after his father's assassination. Driven by a desire to avenge his father and restore honor to his lineage, he rules with a mix of determination and sorrow. Despite his young age, he has proven to be a capable leader, deeply committed to the welfare of his people.

Notes

Zarnaldis carries the heavy burden of his father's legacy and the duty to uncover the truth behind the assassination. He is both a warrior and a king, striving to protect Loch Modar and uphold the ideals his father championed.

Abilities

  • -Divine Smite*: Zarnaldis can expend a spell slot to deal radiant damage to a target in addition to his weapon's damage.
  • -Lay on Hands*: Zarnaldis can heal a total of 90 hit points, divided as he sees fit among creatures he touches.
  • -Aura of Protection*: Zarnaldis and friendly creatures within 10 feet of him gain a +5 bonus to all saving throws.
  • -Champion Challenge*: Zarnaldis can issue a challenge that compels other creatures to do battle with him.
  • -Turn the Tide*: As a bonus action, Zarnaldis can bolster injured creatures within 30 feet of him, restoring hit points to each creature that has less than half its hit points.
  • -Divine Allegiance*: When a creature within 5 feet of Zarnaldis takes damage, he can use his reaction to take the damage instead.
  • -Unyielding Spirit*: Zarnaldis has advantage on saving throws to avoid being paralyzed or stunned.
  • -Exalted Champion*: Zarnaldis can use his action to channel the presence of his deity to grant himself advantage on attack rolls made against a creature within 30 feet.
