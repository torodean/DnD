Background Information

Seraphina Lightbringer is a devout human paladin dedicated to upholding justice and protecting the innocent. Clad in shining plate armor and wielding a righteous longsword, she embodies the ideals of her deity. Seraphina's unwavering faith fuels her divine powers, allowing her to channel holy energy to heal and smite her foes. With her calm demeanor and unwavering resolve, she is a beacon of hope in times of darkness.
