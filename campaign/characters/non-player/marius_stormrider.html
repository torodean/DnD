Background Information

Marius Stormrider, a seasoned warrior and the owner of The Salty Seahorse Inn in Vifavea, welcomes guests with a stoic yet hospitable demeanor. His strong presence and dedication to providing a comfortable stay ensure that visitors enjoy a memorable experience by the seaside. With his battle-hardened skills and keen eye for detail, Marius maintains a well-run establishment that caters to the needs of weary adventurers and locals alike, leaving them with tales of both comfort and valor.
