Background Information

Silas Shadowvale, a mysterious and enigmatic figure, is a human sorcerer who found his calling within the ranks of the Wandering Wanderers Guild . Possessing a deep affinity for shadow magic, Silas weaves his spells with an uncanny darkness that both fascinates and unnerves those around him. His ability to tap into the secrets of the shadow realm allows him to offer telepathic communication and share information with an air of secrecy and mystique. With his brooding presence and unpredictable powers, Silas delves into the forgotten knowledge of ancient civilizations, unearthing arcane truths and unlocking the veiled potential within the realms he traverses.
