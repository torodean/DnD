Background Information

Victorai Eirholm is a powerful elf sorcerer specializing in ice and cold magic, drawing her arcane power from her draconic ancestry tied to a white dragon. With an innate ability to manipulate frost and freeze, Victorai wields spells that chill her enemies to the bone and create icy barriers for defense. Her mastery over cold magic extends beyond combat, allowing her to create intricate ice sculptures and manipulate temperature with precision. Victorai's serene demeanor and sharp intellect make her a respected scholar among her peers, often sought after for her knowledge of ancient magic and lore.

Notes

Victorai wears a crystal pendant infused with ice magic, which enhances her spellcasting abilities and serves as a focus for her cold-based spells.
