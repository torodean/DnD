Background Information

Lyra Nighsho is a Half-Elf ranger, whose proficiency in the ways of nature and her disciplined approach to life makes her an ideal co-worker for Riven Darkwood in The Council of Shadows . Raised in the heart of the Whispering Woods, Lyra's connection to the natural world and her darkvision make her a skilled tracker and scout, perfectly complementing Riven's stealth and deception. With her longbow and short sword, she provides ranged support and is ready for close combat when necessary. As a reliable partner, Lyra's expertise in survival, perception, and nature allows her to navigate challenging terrains, provide valuable insights during reconnaissance missions, and uncover hidden threats. Her calm and level-headed demeanor contrasts well with Riven's cunning and charm, creating a dynamic duo that can adapt to any situation. Together, they form an effective team, each bringing unique strengths to accomplish the Council's secretive and often dangerous missions.
