Background Information

Grix Snaggletoo is a cunning and agile goblin rogue who thrives on the thrill of thievery and deception. Known for his nimble fingers and quick reflexes, Grix specializes in burglary, pickpocketing, and infiltration. His small stature and natural stealth make him adept at navigating tight spaces and evading detection, allowing him to slip in and out of places unnoticed. Grix's chaotic nature and disregard for authority make him a dangerous adversary, always looking for the next lucrative opportunity or daring heist to satisfy his greed and thirst for adventure.

Notes

Grix keeps a collection of stolen trinkets and valuables hidden in various secret compartments throughout his hideout, serving as both trophies and emergency funds.
