Background Information

Shanti Threadneddle is a skilled human tailor known for his meticulous craftsmanship and keen eye for detail. With a passion for fashion and an innate talent for sewing, Shanti has built a successful business in the heart of the city of Suren . His tailor shop is a haven for those seeking custom-made clothing of the highest quality. Shanti takes pride in his work, spending long hours perfecting each garment to ensure a perfect fit and elegant design. Despite his reserved demeanor, Shanti is well-liked among his clientele for his friendly disposition and impeccable taste in fashion.

Notes

Shanti keeps a small sketchbook where he sketches new designs and ideas for future clothing collections, always striving to stay ahead of the latest trends.
