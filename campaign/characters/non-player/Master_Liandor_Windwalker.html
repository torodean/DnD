Background Information

Master Liandor Windwalker is an ancient elf monk who has spent centuries honing his skills in the art of aikido-like martial arts. Renowned for his wisdom and mastery of the body's inner energy, Liandor embodies the principles of balance, harmony, and tranquility. Despite his advanced age and diminutive stature, he moves with grace and precision, effortlessly redirecting the force of his opponents' attacks with fluid movements. Liandor resides in a secluded mountain monastery, where he teaches disciples the art of self-discipline and inner peace through martial arts. His teachings emphasize the interconnectedness of mind, body, and spirit, guiding students on a path of enlightenment and self-discovery.

Notes

Liandor possesses a deep knowledge of herbal remedies and healing techniques, using his expertise to tend to the injuries and ailments of his fellow monks and visitors to the monastery.
