Background Information

Grub Thudashank is a small but sturdy goblin who works as a construction laborer, specializing in building and repairing structures. Despite his soft exterior and simple demeanor, Grub takes pride in his craftsmanship and attention to detail, often impressing his peers with his skillful work. His agility make him well-suited for physical labor, allowing him to handle materials and navigate precarious construction sites with ease. Grub's loyalty to his crewmates and dedication to his craft have earned him respect in the construction industry, where his contributions are valued for their reliability and efficiency.

Notes

Grub carries a small wooden carving of a hammer, a gift from his mentor, which he keeps as a reminder of his journey from apprentice to skilled laborer.
