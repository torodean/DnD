Background Information

Faerwen Grenleaf, the esteemed owner of the Palmhaven Apothecary in Vifavea, is a seasoned healer and devoted servant of the divine. With his profound wisdom and mastery of herbal remedies, Faerwen has become a beacon of hope in the community. As a cleric of the Life domain, he channels the power of his deity to mend wounds, cure ailments, and provide spiritual guidance. His unwavering commitment to the well-being of others has earned him the respect and gratitude of all who seek solace and healing within the walls of the Palmhaven Apothecary.
