Background Information

Lyndara Silferleef is the sister of Elorin Silferleef and shares her family’s connection to the natural world, though her talents lie in the realm of tracking and archery. As a skilled ranger, Lyndara operates in the surrounding wilderness, assisting with the safety of the shipyard by scouting for threats and gathering valuable resources. Her knowledge of the natural environment complements Elorin’s craftsmanship, making them a formidable sibling duo in their respective fields. Her work ensures that the shipyard remains well-supplied and safe from potential dangers.

Notes

Lyndara's role as a ranger enhances her ability to support the shipyard from a different angle, providing essential protection and resources. Her bond with her brother Elorin and her expertise in archery and wilderness skills make her a valuable ally and protector in their shared endeavors.
