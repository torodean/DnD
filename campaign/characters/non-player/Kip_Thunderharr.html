Background Information

Kip Thunderharr is a small but fierce halfling boy who has survived on his own in the wilds since a very young age. With a heart full of courage and a primal rage within him, Kip has learned to fend for himself using brute strength and instinct. Despite his tender years, Kip is already showing signs of becoming a formidable warrior, channeling his rage to overcome obstacles and enemies much larger than himself. His upbringing has made him resilient and resourceful, able to navigate the wilderness with ease and adapt to any challenge that comes his way.

Notes

Kip has a soft spot for animals and often befriends small creatures he encounters in the wild, treating them as his only companions.
