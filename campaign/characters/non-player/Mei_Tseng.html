Background Information

Mei Tseng is the talented and imaginative owner of the Whispering Winds Tattoo Parlor within Ryumon District of Suren, a renowned establishment that specializes in magical and cultural tattoos. Known for her intricate designs and the ability to imbue tattoos with minor enchantments, Mei has built a reputation for her unique and personalized artistry. One of the shop's signature offerings is the "Whispering Wind" tattoo, which allows customers to communicate short messages over long distances with others who bear a similar mark. Mei's expertise and creativity have made her parlor a favorite among those seeking both artistic expression and practical enchantments.

Notes

Mei's deep understanding of magical and cultural symbolism allows her to create tattoos that are not only beautiful but also meaningful. Her "Whispering Wind" tattoos are particularly popular among adventurers and those with loved ones far away. Despite her success, Mei remains humble and dedicated to her craft, always striving to perfect her art.
