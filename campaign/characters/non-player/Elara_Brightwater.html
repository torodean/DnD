Background Information

Elara Brightwater is a devoted cleric of the life domain, dedicated to serving her deity and aiding those in need. With a compassionate heart and unwavering faith, Elara travels far and wide to offer healing and guidance to the sick, injured, and downtrodden. Her soothing presence and gentle demeanor make her a comforting presence in times of hardship. Elara's faith in the power of life and healing drives her to confront darkness and bring light to those who are suffering. Despite the challenges she faces, Elara remains steadfast in her mission to spread hope and compassion wherever she goes.

Notes

Elara wears a simple yet elegant amulet around her neck, adorned with the symbol of her deity, which she holds dear as a symbol of her faith and devotion.
