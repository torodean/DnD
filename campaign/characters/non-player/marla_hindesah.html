Background Information

Marla Hindesah, a charismatic and warm-hearted human bard, is the esteemed tavern keeper in the province of Eldara . Known for her hospitality and exceptional ability to create a welcoming atmosphere, Marla's establishment is a sanctuary amidst the desert sands. Her melodious voice, accompanied by the strumming of her lute, fills the tavern with enchanting tunes, captivating patrons from near and far. With her insightful nature and intuitive understanding of people's needs, Marla ensures that every guest feels valued and cared for. Her tavern has become a popular gathering spot where travelers and locals alike find solace, camaraderie, and good cheer.
