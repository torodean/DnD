Background Information

Zara Ethrax is the wise and enigmatic owner of Spirit Wind Antiques in the Ryumon District of Suren, a quaint shop filled with rare and unusual antiques. With an aura of mystery, Zara has curated a fascinating collection that ranges from ancient relics to curious trinkets, each item holding its own story. Her deep knowledge of history and magic allows her to identify and appraise the most obscure and valuable items, attracting collectors and adventurers alike to her shop. Customers come to Spirit Wind Antiques not just for the items on sale, but also for Zara's wisdom and insight into the past.

Notes

Zara's background as a sage and her proficiency in divination magic make her an exceptional antiquarian. She has an uncanny ability to uncover the hidden histories and magical properties of the items in her collection, making her shop a treasure trove of knowledge and wonder. The shop's mysterious aura and Zara's charismatic presence draw in those who seek not only rare items but also the stories and secrets they hold.
