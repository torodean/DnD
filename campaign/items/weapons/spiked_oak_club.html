Spiked Oak Club

A hefty club carved from solid oak, studded with crude iron spikes along one side. Deals 1d4 bludgeoning damage plus 1 piercing damage on a hit. Its rugged construction makes it durable but unwieldy, imposing a -1 penalty to attack rolls for users without proficiency in simple weapons. Costs 2 gp.
