The Fang Of Azshira

Source: Created by Antonius Torode
Caption: The Fang Of Azshira.

Summary

Behold the legendary Fang of Azshira, one among a distinguished set of blades that includes the illustrious Fangs of Pellinore, Elaine, and Essieldore. Crafted from a pristine crystal infused with ancient enchantments, this exquisite weapon emanates a delicate mist of ethereal dark red hues. Its crystal composition boasts a mesmerizing transparency, revealing glimpses of the inner workings that lie within.

Forged in perfect harmony with its complementary counterpart, the Fang of Azshira cradles within its core the very essence of Azshira Loredmeyer's soul, intricately intertwined with the embodiment of her extraordinary abilities. As this mystical dagger graces the hands of its chosen bearer, the soul residing within forms an unbreakable bond, growing ever more connected to its guardian. The profoundness of this connection unlocks a multitude of diverse capabilities, contingent upon the wielder's moral compass. The Fang of Azshira stands as an enduring testament to its legacyâ€”a blade of unparalleled finesse and supernatural influence. Its crystal marvels captivate the beholder, while the ethereal soul within pulses with untapped potential.

Damage

This weapon is considered a dagger and does 1d4 piercing damage and 1d4 Flame damage. After becoming increasingly attuned to the weapon and the soul it contains, the soul within this weapon begins to allow its influence to help you in battle and gains the following effects.

  • Level 3: Gains +1 to all attack rolls.
  • Level 6: Gains the Tapped Soul Effect (See below)
  • Level 9: Gains the Soul Flame Bite (See below)
  • Level 12: Gains the Improved Tapped Soul Effect (See below)
  • Level 15: Gains the Improved Soul Flame Bite (See below)
  • Level 18: This weapon gains 1d8 Flame damage on each hit.

Rightous Wielders

From the depths of virtuous alignments springs forth extraordinary potential, empowering the Fang of Azshira with unparalleled prowess. Harness the Tapped Soul effect, allowing the blade to draw upon the fiery energies of adversaries, replenishing and rejuvenating the bearer in the heat of battle. Furthermore, the ethereal touch of the Soul Flame Bite enchants each strike, engulfing the target's essence in an incendiary blaze. Once per turn, the afflicted foe endures additional Flame damage, while subsequent attacks further intensify the infernal torment inflicted upon the target.

Tapped Soul (Effect)

With each strike, you have the ability to siphon 1d4 Flame damage from the enemy, thereby healing yourself.

Improved Tapped Soul (Effect)

The Tapped soul effect gains an extra 1d4 (Increasing to a total of 2d4).

Soul Flame Bite

When executing a melee attack with this weapon, you possess the power to sear the soul of the target, inflicting a molten burn that lasts for one hour or until dispelled. The target will suffer an extra 1d4 fire damage once per turn on melee attacks made against it. If this effect is already active on the target, subsequent melee attacks can further intensify the scorching torment, dealing an additional 1d4 damage.

Improved Soul Flame Bite

The Soul Flame Bite gains an extra 1d4 (Increasing to a total of 2d4).

Neutral Wielders

Those of neutral alignment face an imperative choice. Embrace the virtuous path, reveling in the aforementioned effects, or succumb to a darker allure, embracing the sinister potential that thrives within. This choice cannot be undone once made.

Wicked Wielders

The wicked shall wield the Tapped Soul effect with fearsome potency, causing the Fang of Azshira to unleash relentless Flame damage upon their enemies. Meanwhile, the touch of the Soul Flame Bite consumes their targets with an unyielding grasp of scorching intensity, delivering molten torment and debilitating their movements.

Tapped Soul (Effect)

On each strike of the weapon, you deal 1d8 Flame damage to the enemy.

Improved Tapped Soul (Effect)

The Tapped soul effect gains an extra 1d8 (Increasing to a total of 2d8).

Soul Flame Bite

When executing a melee attack with this weapon, you possess the power to sear the soul of the target, inflicting a molten burn that lasts for one hour or until dispelled. The target will suffer an extra 1d4 fire damage once per turn on melee attacks made against it with this weapon. If this effect is already active on the target, subsequent melee attacks can further intensify the scorching torment, dealing an additional 1d4 damage.

Improved Soul Flame Bite

The Soul Flame Bite gains an extra 1d4 (Increasing to a total of 2d4).
