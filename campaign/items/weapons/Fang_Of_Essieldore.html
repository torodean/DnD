Fang Of Essieldore

The Fang Of Essieldore

Source: Created by Antonius Torode
Caption: The Fang Of Essieldore.

Summary

Behold the legendary Fang of Essieldore, one among a distinguished set of blades that includes the illustrious Fang Of Pellinore, Fang Of Azshira, and Fang Of Elaine . Crafted from a pristine crystal infused with ancient enchantments, this exquisite weapon emanates a delicate mist of ethereal green hues. Its crystal composition boasts a mesmerizing transparency, revealing glimpses of the inner workings that lie within.

Forged in harmony with its counterpart, the Fang of Essieldore cradles within its core the very essence of Essieldore Loredmeyer's soul, intertwined with the embodiment of his extraordinary abilities. As this mystical dagger graces the hands of its bearer, the soul residing within grows ever more connected to its chosen guardian. The depths of this connection unlock a wealth of diverse capabilities, contingent upon the moral compass that guides the wielder. The Fang of Essieldore stands as a testament to its legacy, a blade of extraordinary finesse and supernatural influence. Its crystal marvels captivate the eye, while the ethereal soul within pulses with untapped potential.

Damage

This weapon is considered a dagger and does 1d4 piercing damage and 1d4 poison damage. After becoming increasingly attuned to the weapon and the soul it contains, the soul within this weapon begins to allow its influence to help you in battle and gains the following effects.

  • Level 3: Gains +1 to all attack rolls.
  • Level 6: Gains the Tapped Soul Effect (See below)
  • Level 9: Gains the Poison Bite (See below)
  • Level 12: Gains the Improved Tapped Soul Effect (See below)
  • Level 15: Gains the Improved Poison Bite (See below)
  • Level 18: This weapon gains 1d8 Poison damage on each hit.

Rightous Wielders

From righteous alignments springs forth extraordinary potential, empowering the Fang of Essieldore with unparalleled prowess. Harness the Tapped Soul effect, allowing the blade to siphon energies from adversaries, replenishing and rejuvenating the bearer in the heat of combat. Moreover, the ethereal touch of the poison Bite enchants each strike, enveloping the target's essence in a sinister poison. Once per turn, the inflicted foe endures additional poison damage, while subsequent attacks further weaken their movements, clouding their minds.

Tapped Soul (Effect)

On each strike of the weapon, you can choose to siphon 1d4 poison damage from the enemy and heal yourself.

Improved Tapped Soul (Effect)

The Tapped soul effect gains an extra 1d4 (Increasing to a total of 2d4).

Poison Bite

When you make a melee attack with this weapon you can choose to inflict a poison to the target. This lasts for one minute or until cured. The target will take 1d4 extra poison damage once per turn on a melee attack against it. If this effect is already on the target, you can choose to further poison the target on a subsequent melee attack, which will decrease the targets concentration next round by removing the ability to take any bonus actions.

Improved Poison Bite

The Poison Bite effect gains an extra 1d4 (Increasing to a total of 2d4).

Neutral Wielders

For those of neutral alignment, an imperative choice must be made. Embrace the virtuous path and revel in the aforementioned effects, or succumb to a darker allure, embracing the sinister potential that thrives within. This choice cannot be undone once made.

Wicked Wielders

The wicked shall wield the Tapped Soul effect with a fearsome potency, causing the Fang Of Elaine to unleash relentless frost damage upon their enemies. Meanwhile, the touch of the Soul Frost Bite consumes their targets with an unyielding frosty grip, delivering frigid torment and debilitating their movements.

Tapped Soul (Effect)

On each strike of the weapon, you deal 1d8 poison damage to the enemy.

Improved Tapped Soul (Effect)

The Tapped soul effect gains an extra 1d8 (Increasing to a total of 2d8).

Poison Bite

When you make a melee attack with this weapon you inflict a poison into the body of the target. This lasts for one minute or until cured. The target will take 1d4 extra poison damage once per turn on a melee attack against it. If this effect is already on the target, you further poison the target on a subsequent melee attack, which will decrease the targets concentration next round by removing the ability to take any bonus actions.

Improved Poison Bite

The Poison Bite effect gains an extra 1d4 (Increasing to a total of 2d4).
