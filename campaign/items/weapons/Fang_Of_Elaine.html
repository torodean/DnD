Fang Of Elaine

The Fang Of Elaine

Source: Created by Antonius Torode
Caption: The Fang Of Elaine.

Summary

Behold the legendary Fang of Elaine, one among a distinguished set of blades that includes the illustrious Fangs of Pellinore, Azshira, and Essieldore. Crafted from a pristine crystal infused with ancient enchantments, this exquisite weapon emanates a delicate mist of ethereal light blue hues. Its crystal composition boasts a mesmerizing transparency, revealing glimpses of the inner workings that lie within.

Forged in harmony with its counterpart, the Fang of Elaine cradles within its core the very essence of Elaine Loredmeyer's soul, intertwined with the embodiment of her extraordinary abilities. As this mystical dagger graces the hands of its bearer, the soul residing within grows ever more connected to its chosen guardian. The depths of this connection unlock a wealth of diverse capabilities, contingent upon the moral compass that guides the wielder. The Fang of Elaine stands as a testament to its legacy, a blade of extraordinary finesse and supernatural influence. Its crystal marvels captivate the eye, while the ethereal soul within pulses with untapped potential.

Damage

This weapon is considered a dagger and does 1d4 piercing damage and 1d4 frost damage. After becoming increasingly attuned to the weapon and the soul it contains, the soul within this weapon begins to allow its influence to help you in battle and gains the following effects.

  • Level 3: Gains +1 to all attack rolls.
  • Level 6: Gains the Tapped Soul Effect (See below)
  • Level 9: Gains the Soul Frost Bite (See below)
  • Level 12: Gains the Improved Tapped Soul Effect (See below)
  • Level 15: Gains the Improved Soul Frost Bite (See below)
  • Level 18: This weapon gains 1d8 frost damage on each hit.

Rightous Wielders

From righteous alignments springs forth extraordinary potential, empowering the Fang of Elaine with unparalleled prowess. Harness the Tapped Soul effect, allowing the blade to siphon frosty energies from adversaries, replenishing and rejuvenating the bearer in the heat of combat. Moreover, the ethereal touch of the Soul Frost Bite enchants each strike, enveloping the target's essence in a chilling frost. Once per turn, the inflicted foe endures additional frost damage, while subsequent attacks further constrict their movements, slowing them to a sluggish crawl.

Tapped Soul (Effect)

On each strike of the weapon, you can choose to siphon 1d4 frost damage from the enemy and heal yourself.

Improved Tapped Soul (Effect)

The Tapped soul effect gains an extra 1d4 (Increasing to a total of 2d4).

Soul Frost Bite

When you make a melee attack with this weapon you can choose to inflict a Frosty chill to the soul of the target. This lasts for one hour or until dispelled. The target will take 1d4 extra frost damage once per turn on a melee attack against it with this weapon. If this effect is already on the target, you can choose to further chill the targets soul on a subsequent melee attack, which will decrease the targets movement by half next round.

Improved Soul Frost Bite

The Soul Frost Bite gains an extra 1d4 (Increasing to a total of 2d4).

Neutral Wielders

For those of neutral alignment, an imperative choice must be made. Embrace the virtuous path and revel in the aforementioned effects, or succumb to a darker allure, embracing the sinister potential that thrives within. This choice cannot be undone once made.

Wicked Wielders

The wicked shall wield the Tapped Soul effect with a fearsome potency, causing the Fang of Elaine to unleash relentless frost damage upon their enemies. Meanwhile, the touch of the Soul Frost Bite consumes their targets with an unyielding frosty grip, delivering frigid torment and debilitating their movements.

Tapped Soul (Effect)

On each strike of the weapon, you deal 1d8 frost damage to the enemy.

Improved Tapped Soul (Effect)

The Tapped soul effect gains an extra 1d8 (Increasing to a total of 2d8).

Soul Frost Bite

When you make a melee attack with this weapon you inflict a Frosty chill to the soul of the target. This lasts for one hour or until dispelled. The target will take 1d4 extra frost damage once per turn on a melee attack against it. If this effect is already on the target, you further chill the targets soul on a subsequent melee attack, which will decrease the targets movement by half next round.

Improved Soul Frost Bite

The Soul Frost Bite gains an extra 1d4 (Increasing to a total of 2d4).
