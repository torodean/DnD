Bone-Tipped Spear

A simple wooden spear with a sharpened bone head, lashed tightly with sinew. Deals 1d6 piercing damage (or 1d8 if used two-handed), with a range of 20/60 ft when thrown. Primitive but effective, it costs 1 gp and weighs 3 lbs, appealing to hunters or tribal warriors.
