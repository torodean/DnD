Iron-Plated Brigandine

A flexible vest of canvas lined with small, overlapping iron plates, riveted for durability. Grants AC 14 + Dex modifier (max 2), weighs 20 lbs, and costs 50 gp. Its construction allows for quiet movement, reducing the Stealth disadvantage penalty to -5 instead of the usual -10.
