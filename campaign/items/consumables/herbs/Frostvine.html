Basic Information

Nestled amidst the snow-covered peaks of Bakaria, the Frostvine is a remarkable herb that thrives in the frigid embrace of the high altitudes. Its distinctive blue leaves, resembling delicate ice crystals, stand out against the wintry landscape. When touched, the leaves feel cool to the skin, as if carrying the essence of the frost-kissed air.

The Frostvine is highly valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in the realm of pain relief and healing. The extracts obtained from its leaves and stems possess a natural numbing effect, which makes it a valuable ingredient in the creation of salves, ointments, and tinctures. These topical remedies are known to alleviate discomfort, ease muscular aches and strains, and reduce inflammation.

Herbalists and healers of Bakaria have long recognized the power of the Frostvine in soothing various ailments. Its cooling properties make it particularly effective in treating burns, sunburns, and insect bites, providing instant relief from pain and irritation. The vine's extracts also possess mild antispasmodic properties, making it useful for relaxing tense muscles and relieving cramps.

The Frostvine's ability to thrive in the harshest of conditions, where icy winds howl and snow blankets the land, is believed to contribute to its healing properties. It is said that the plant absorbs the essence of the cold and transforms it into a soothing balm, allowing individuals to find comfort even in the midst of adversity.

Harvesting the Frostvine requires a delicate touch and a deep understanding of its natural habitat. Gatherers venture into the higher altitudes, equipped with warm attire to withstand the freezing temperatures. Carefully plucking the leaves and stems, they ensure that the plant remains undisturbed, allowing it to continue its growth and contribute to the delicate balance of the Bakarian ecosystem.

Travelers and adventurers who journey through the snowy peaks of Bakaria often seek out the Frostvine, drawn by its reputation as a natural remedy for pain and inflammation. Whether it is an icy windburn or a strained muscle from an arduous trek, the soothing touch of the Frostvine's extracts provides respite and rejuvenation.
