Bitterroot

A gnarled, dark brown root found in rocky hillsides, known for its intensely bitter taste and stamina-boosting properties. A single root (enough for one tonic) costs 1 gp and weighs 2 oz.

Recipe

  • Locate bitterroot plants in dry, rocky soil, identified by their small, purple flowers.
  • Dig carefully around the base to extract the root whole, avoiding breakage.
  • Rinse off dirt and dry in the sun for 2 days to preserve its potency.
