Cloudcap Mushroom

A rare, gray-capped mushroom that thrives in damp caves, known for enhancing perception when processed. One mushroom (enough for one extract) costs 2 gp and weighs 3 oz.

Recipe

  • Search for cloudcap mushrooms in dark, moist caverns, growing on rocky walls.
  • Cut the caps cleanly with a knife, leaving the stem to regrow.
  • Dry the caps in a ventilated bag for 3 days to prevent rot while retaining potency.
