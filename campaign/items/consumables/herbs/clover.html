Clover

A specific strain of wild clover with broad, soft leaves, prized for its calming effects when brewed. A handful (enough for one elixir) costs 5 sp and weighs 1 oz.

Recipe

  • Harvest clover from shady meadows, selecting the broadest, greenest leaves.
  • Pluck leaves gently to avoid crushing them, gathering at dawn for best freshness.
  • Dry the leaves in a cool, dark place for 1 day to maintain their soothing qualities.
