Honeyed Locust Cakes

Sweet, crunchy cakes made from roasted locusts coated in wild honey, a delicacy in arid regions. A batch of 3 cakes costs 2 gp and provides a quick burst of energy (equivalent to half a dayâ€™s rations).

Recipe

  • Roast 1 cup of cleaned locusts over an open flame until crispy, about 10 minutes.
  • Melt 3 tablespoons of wild honey with a pinch of salt in a small pot.
  • Toss the locusts in the honey mixture, spread on a flat surface, and let cool until hardened.
