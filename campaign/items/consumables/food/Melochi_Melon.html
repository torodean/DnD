Melochini

Melochi Melon, is an exotic fruit that grows in the Da'Chika Isles and area's surrounding Suren . This fruit has a soft, edible skin similar to that of a papaya texture. The fruit is somewhat melonous in texture but is very soft. The Melochi Melon primarily differs from the Melochini in its size and juiciness. The Melochi Melon is not nearly as juicy as the Melochini even though it is very closely related to it genetically. The Melochini seeds are similar to that of pumpkin seeds, but slightly harder.
