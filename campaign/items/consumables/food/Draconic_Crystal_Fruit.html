Draconic Crystal Fruit is an exotic fruit that grows with a hard skin resembling dracon eggs (believed to be a natural defense mechanism to deter predators). Under the skin is a jelly like fruit that is similar to a lychee. The jelly part grows in crystalline structures with one large seed in the center of the fruit. This fruit is found in tropical regions with a lot of rain such as the Da'Chika Isles .
