Glemure Tendril Cactus

The Glemure Tendril Cactus is an exotic breed of cactus that can grow with or without spikes. This rare cactii has a unique gender dichotomy, where the male species will grow spikes, and the female will grow flowers and spread pollon and seeds. It has a thick skin which contains a dragonfruit like center. This center part is regularly consumed and considered a high class fruit. This fruit grows in the Da'Chika Isles and can sometimes also be found in other areas surrounding Suren .
