Melochini

Melochini, often referred to as simply Melochi, is an exotic fruit that grows in the Da'Chika Isles and area's surrounding Suren . This fruit has a soft, edible skin similar to that of a papaya texture. The fruit is somewhat melonous in texture but is very soft and juicy like a kiwi. The Melochini is very closely related to the Melochi Melon . the Melochi seeds are very small and hard.
