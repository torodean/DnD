Desert Sage Infusion

Enjoy this soothing infusion as a calming drink or use it as a base for other recipes.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon dried Desert Sage leaves
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions

  • Place the dried Desert Sage leaves in a teapot or a heatproof container.
  • Pour boiling water over the leaves.
  • Cover and let it steep for about 10 minutes.
  • Strain the infusion into a cup.
  • Optionally, you can add a teaspoon of honey or lemon juice for taste.
