Soothing clover Elixir

A pale green mixture in a stoppered flask, made from clover and mint. When consumed, it calms the nerves, granting advantage on saving throws against being frightened for 30 minutes. Costs 3 gp per flask.

Recipe

  • Crush 2 handfuls of fresh clover leaves and 1 handful of mint into a paste.
  • Steep the paste in 1 cup of hot water for 15 minutes.
  • Filter through cloth, add a pinch of honey, and pour into a flask.
