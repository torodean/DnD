pine resin Balm

A thick, amber-colored salve in a small tin, made from pine sap and oils. Applying it to minor wounds stabilizes the user (as if succeeding on a death saving throw) if theyâ€™re at 0 hit points. Costs 2 gp per tin, single use.

Recipe

  • Melt 1 ounce of pine resin with 1 tablespoon of lard over low heat.
  • Stir in 1 teaspoon of crushed lavender until fully blended.
  • Pour into a tin and let harden for 4 hours before sealing.
