Cloudcap Extract

A thin, smoky-gray fluid in a glass phial, derived from rare mushrooms. When ingested, it sharpens the senses, granting a +2 bonus to Wisdom (Perception) checks for 1 hour. Costs 6 gp per phial, but causes mild dizziness afterward.

Recipe

  • Slice 2 cloudcap mushroom thinly and soak in 1 cup of warm water for 1 hour.
  • Boil the mixture down to half its volume, about 20 minutes.
  • Cool, filter out solids, and store in a glass phial with a tight stopper.
