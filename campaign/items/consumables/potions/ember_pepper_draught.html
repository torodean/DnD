ember pepper Draught

A spicy, red-orange liquid in a clay bottle, distilled from fiery peppers. Drinking it warms the body, providing resistance to cold weather effects (not damage) for 2 hours. Costs 4 gp per bottle and leaves the drinker sweating profusely.

Recipe

  • Dry and grind 3 ember pepper into a fine powder.
  • Mix the powder with 1 cup of strong grain alcohol and let sit for 2 days.
  • Strain the mixture, dilute with 1/2 cup of water, and bottle securely.
