Tol'Medith

Tol'Medith, the sacred Temple of the Morning Star

Tol'Medith, the sacred Temple of the Morning Star, stands as a remarkable pyramid-like structure within the lost city of Tol'Kahtan . From above, it presents a unique sight, with its four overlapping squares forming twelve corners and sixteen sides on the bottom face. Instead of tapering to a point like a conventional pyramid, Tol'Medith features a majestic upper structure adorned with a sprawling platform and enchanting garden areas. Crowned by a hollow ceiling structure that culminates in a pointed tip, the temple boasts a breathtaking illusionary dragon that emanates a magical breath effect, casting an illuminating glow upon its surroundings.

Access to Tol'Medith's interior is shrouded in mystery, as the ancient entrance has long been sealed. However, hidden underground tunnels connected to the city of Tol'Kahtan provide the only known passage into the temple's depths. Within its hallowed halls, a myriad of sections awaits discovery. At the heart of the structure lies the main chamber, featuring a grand central pillar surrounded by four chambers—dedicated to honor, nature, dragons, and the undead—on each side. The chamber floor is crafted from transparent crystal material, offering glimpses of the vast garden area that sprawls beneath, teeming with magical creatures and natural wonders.

Ascending from the central pillar, the main chamber opens up to reveal glass walls that grant views of the second floor—a sprawling library brimming with a wealth of knowledge. Above the library, smaller chambers house laboratories, living quarters, and various other functional spaces. Adorned with relics, statues, artifacts, intricately carved maps, and stunning architecture, the main chamber exudes an aura of reverence and awe. The ambience is dimly lit, with concealed lights scattered throughout the structure, waiting to be activated by those who possess the knowledge to do so.

Tol'Medith stands as a testament to the advanced craftsmanship and ingenuity of the ancient civilization that once thrived within Tol'Kahtan . Its magnificent design and hidden treasures continue to beckon adventurers, scholars, and seekers of ancient wisdom, promising an immersive exploration of its ethereal beauty and profound mysteries.
