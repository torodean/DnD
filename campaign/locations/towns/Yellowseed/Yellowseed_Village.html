Yellowseed Village

Vifavea

Source: Created by Antonius Torode
Caption: Yellowseed.

Summary

A small town within The Ashenlands, tightly-knit with a population of 1008 residents. The community members share a close bond, familiar with one another due to the town's intimate size. To the north lies expansive farmland, sprawling across the Forestes area. Several townsfolk have made these farms their homes, making occasional trips to the town for leisure or essential provisions. Strategically situated on the shores of the Tyrex Bay Bay, the town serves as a bustling hub for traders. Its prime location attracts a constant influx of merchants and traders, contributing to its vibrant and dynamic atmosphere. Amid this backdrop, a few notable villagers stand out, each with their own unique stories and roles within the community.
