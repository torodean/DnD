Saltahati Hill

Saltahati Hill is an elevated, prestigious area in the heart of Suren, where the Sultan and other royal families reside. This region is characterized by its two main rises: a lower rise that mostly surrounds the higher one. At the top level, where the Sultan's grand palace is located, there is a serene pond with a picturesque waterfall that flows down to Sura Lake, the main lake in the center of Suren . The lower level is home to other royal families and is adorned with luxurious mansions, lush gardens, and elegant courtyards.
