timeline

About

This timeline uses the DMestros time system. It is a collection of events that happen pertaining to DnD campaigns that I have ran.

-52938

52,934 years ago, the ancient Dreamspawn known as Yltharis threatened all life on the planet.

00000

In the year 0, A group of explorers set forth to obtain three rare artifacts known as the Trinity Stones. With the rise of a Celestial Religion threatening world domination, a great Neakoros awakened only to assist the explorers. These explorers ultimately failed, though the Grand wizard Baba, along with the Malitia of Aurushire succeeded in obtaining the stones and stopping the Celestial Threat. This is the time period when Baba learned of the means of creating an artificial diety.

00314

In the year of 314, A group of explorers set out for a small town of Dessaquest . After a brief adventure, they found themselves imprisoned on a prison colony. After a while, they discovered a grand and hidden cave while digging the mines deep within a mountain. These adventurers unearthed a time-lost artifact. The adventurers nearly managed to escape, only to be betrayed by someone they thought they could trust. the artifact fell into the posession of the guards, only to later be stolen by pirates off a cargo ship.

00322

In the year 322, A group of explorers obtained the rare puzzle box of Zytharoth near a small town of Yellowseed. Their fate unfortunately fell captive to it's dark powers. The artifact remained in confinement for a few years until it was linked to a mysterious mist just 3 years later.

00325

In the year 325, A great Ethereal Mist enveloped all of the land.

00330

In the year 330, The great ethereal mist mysteriously dissipated. milton manastorm was captured for his apparent shinanigins involving the missing Ambassador of Vifavea . A group of adventurers hired as legal advisers to Milton uncovered the artifact that had caused the mist and attempted to deliver it to the Grand Wizard baba - only to never be seen again.

00441

In the year 441, the Puzzle Box Of Zytharoth (an artifact that had caused a great world mist about a hundred years proir) was unveiled in the Desert of Eldara . The artifact made its way to the city of Suren, where it was sealed away in a deep vault within the earth and studied carefully. The study of the artifact revealed ancient powers looming within the city of Suren that were deeply connected to the artifact.

00417

A group of adventurers were tasked with missions leading to the acquisition of the seven antianima stones by a mysterious customer.
