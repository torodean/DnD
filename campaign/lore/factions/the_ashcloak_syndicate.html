the ashcloak syndicate

Overview

The Ashcloak Syndicate is a sprawling network of thieves, smugglers, and informants that operates in the underbelly of major cities. Known for their signature ash-gray cloaks, they blend into the soot and smoke of urban life, striking deals and disappearing without a trace. Their influence is subtle but pervasive, with tendrils in black markets and corrupt officials alike.

Leadership

  • Mira (Ashveil) Tormen: A cunning human rogue who founded the Syndicate.
  • Korvax the Silent: A tiefling enforcer who speaks only in gestures.
  • Lirien Duskwind: An elven spymaster with eyes in every tavern.

Operations

The Syndicate specializes in the following operations:

  • They smuggle rare goods—arcane artifacts, forbidden tomes, and exotic beasts.
  • They bribe guards to look the other way.
  • They run a network of street urchins as messengers.
  • They sabotage rival gangs’ shipments.

Their biggest score was stealing a dragon’s egg from a merchant prince, though its whereabouts remain unknown.
