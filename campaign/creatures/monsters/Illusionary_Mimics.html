Illusionary Mimics

Creatures capable of mimicking the appearance of the illusionary landscape. When detected, they typically appear as a familiar creature such as slugs or snails. They use this ability to lure unsuspecting prey or adventurers into traps. Skilled illusionists may seek them out as companions or familiars.
