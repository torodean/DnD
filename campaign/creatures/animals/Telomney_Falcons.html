Telomney Falcons

The Telomney Falcons are large falcon-like creatures. They range in size from small to humanoid sized birds. They feed off small critters, reptiles, and will scavange other animals if small critters are not abundant. These falcons are extremely intelligent and often have some birds spawn that are significantly smarter than the rest - exhibiting humanoid like characteristics.
